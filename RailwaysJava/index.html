Rules

The aim of the game is to make a connected railway track that goes through every tile on the board. You can achieve this by building/destroying tracks on empty tiles.

Controls

You can left-click on a tile to build a track on it. If there are multiple options each left-click will cycle through the options. You can remove a track on a tile by right-clicking on it.

Tiles

Empty

You can place any type of track.

Oasis

You cannot place tracks on this tile.

Bridge

You can only place straight tracks in the direction of the bridge.

Mountain

You can only place curved tracks in the direction of the canyon.
